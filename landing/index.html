Why BidReel

You shoot all day. Then you lose the night to a quote.

Most great videographers lose work in the gap between the discovery call and the proposal. The client is excited. Then they wait. The momentum cools while the estimate sits half-finished in a Google Doc. BidReel closes that gap — a polished proposal goes out while the conversation is still warm.
