Delete Your BidReel Account

Operated by Amerisold LLC (dba AmeriFilms) · bidreel.io

You can permanently delete your BidReel account and all of your data at any time. There are two ways to do it.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (fastest)

  1. Open the BidReel app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Settings tab.
  3. Scroll to the Account section and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and data are erased immediately.

Option 2 — Request by email

Email souley@amerifilms.io from the address tied to your account, with the subject "Delete my BidReel account." We will verify the request and delete your account within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your profile (name, company, phone)
  • Every proposal, bid, client name, and project detail you created

What we keep, and for how long

Once your account is deleted, your personal data and proposals are removed from our systems. We may retain limited transaction and billing records where required by law or by the Apple App Store / Google Play for their standard retention periods. We do not keep your proposals or profile after deletion.

Questions about deletion or your data? Contact souley@amerifilms.io.
